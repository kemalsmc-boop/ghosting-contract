The Terms — Articles I through VI

The Official
Clauses

I
The Seven-Day Notice Clause Enforced
Kemal Rahman agrees, solemnly and in writing, to provide Juliet Nguyen with no fewer than seven (7) calendar days advance notice before any ghosting event commences. Said notice shall be delivered clearly via text or call. The Ghost may not initiate radio silence without this notice. Any unauthorized ghost is null and void.
II
The Emotional Confusion Protection Clause Critical
The Recipient shall not engage in acts of deliberate or accidental emotional confusion, including:
(a) laughing at something the way only she laughs at things;
(b) being vulnerable, kind, or genuine at the wrong moment;
(c) saying something that is just very her at a pivotal moment;
(d) existing in a way that makes the Ghost reconsider everything.

Violation of this clause voids any ghost attempt within 48 hours. The Ghost cannot be held responsible for wavering caused by Julie being Julie. This clause acknowledges, formally, that she already broke it before this contract was written.
III
The Nature of This Ghost Indefinite
This ghost is indefinite in duration. It is not a pause with a planned end date. It is not a soft launch. It exists for as long as it needs to, under the terms described in Article IV below.
IV
Permitted Exceptions to the Ghost Binding
The ghost may only be lifted under the following specific conditions:
(a) The Motorcycle Exception: Should the Recipient decide to buy a motorcycle, she may contact the Ghost for assistance in testing it. The Ghost will show up. He will also worry about her the entire time.

(b) The Logan Occasion: One night out with Logan Moreland, her cousin, his friend, for drinks, like old times. This exception is pre-approved, non-transferable, and limited to one occurrence per ghost period. It may feel completely normal. That is allowed.

(c) The Only Exception That Really Matters: The ghost is lifted, fully, permanently, and joyfully, the moment she feels ready for something real. No notice required. No clause to invoke. Just say so.

Hard restriction: The ghost may not be broken if the Ghost is in a relationship. Respect is a two-way clause.
V
Impact on Emotional Wellbeing Critical
If the Recipient breaks the ghost outside of the permitted exceptions listed in Article IV, she does so with full acknowledgment that Kemal's emotional wellbeing is a protected interest under this Agreement. The ghost exists for a reason. Breaking it carelessly would cause emotional harm, for which the Ghost will owe the Recipient $20 and one sincere conversation.
VI
The Julie Exception Permanent
Notwithstanding all prior clauses, this contract formally recognizes the Julie Exception, an irrevocable legal principle stating that Juliet Nguyen specifically makes everything harder than it is supposed to be due to her stubborn nature. The Ghost accepts full liability for having feelings regardless. He also accepts that he would do it again. This clause is non-negotiable, cannot be amended, and will probably come up again someday.
